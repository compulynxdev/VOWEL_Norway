##  About

**VOWEL** (Visitors' Official Welcome) is a digital visitor management system by **CompuLynx**. It replaces traditional logbooks with a fast, secure, and paperless front-desk experience.

Deployed across commercial and residential premises in 🇰🇪 **Kenya**, 🇺🇬 **Uganda**, and 🇹🇿 **Tanzania**, VOWEL ensures seamless check-ins, instant visitor tracking, and fully digital records — bringing reception and security operations into the modern era.

---

##  Key Features

| | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 📝 | **Paperless Check-ins** | Visitors self-register or are quickly logged at the front desk, generating a fast digital footprint the moment they arrive. |
| 🔐 | **Access Control** | Integrates into your facility's security infrastructure to enhance both visitor experience and physical safety. |
| ☁️ | **Cloud & Digital Records** | Secure, searchable databases that improve building transparency and support compliance requirements. |
| ⚡ | **Instant Tracking** | Real-time visibility into who's on-site, when they arrived, and who they're visiting. |
| 🌍 | **Multi-Region Ready** | Proven across commercial and residential deployments in East Africa. |
